About Us

Junior Coders Learning Centre provides a creative, inspiring and collaborative environment to learn Computer Science. It is our goal to inspire students to create technology - not just consume technology.

Mission

We strive to develop and deliver the highest quality, most inventive technology experiences to the next generation. We provide a fun learning environment where students are empowered with the skills they need to be successful, ethical, effective problem-solvers and life-long learners who will contribute positively to the world.




OPEN UP A NEW WORLD TO INTELLIGENT, CREATIVE CHILDREN

Young children are intrigued and excited by technology, and this is an ideal time for them to get involved in the creative process of coding. Our Big Kids after school program creates an immersive, activity-based fun learning environment exposing your child to a wide spectrum of computer concepts.


PROGRAM GOALS

Learning coding helps develop young minds for grasping more advanced concepts later on and opens up avenues for problem solving, creative thinking, innovation, and the skill to design of systems for specific tasks. It also fosters an understanding of the power and limits of human and machine intelligences.
